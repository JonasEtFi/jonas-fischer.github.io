Favourite Project

LVSM-VAE: Transformer-Based Novel View Synthesis in Latent Space

Adapts LVSM to operate in a VAE latent space so we can process longer camera sequences with less memory while keeping photoreal novel views. The transformer works on patchified latent tokens conditioned with scaled camera embeddings, then decodes the prediction back to RGB.
